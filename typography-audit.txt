PART 5: CLASSIFICATION LEGEND
================================================================================

PAGE-HERO: Large page titles (40-48px, Space Grotesk, bold)
SECTION-HEADER: Major section dividers (24-32px, Space Grotesk, semibold)
SUBSECTION-HEADER: Minor section dividers (24px, Space Grotesk, semibold)
CARD-TITLE: Component headers (16-18px, Space Grotesk, semibold)
BODY-PARAGRAPH: Primary content (14px, Manrope, normal)
BODY-LARGE: Emphasized body (18px, Manrope, normal)
METADATA: Secondary info (12px, Manrope, normal, muted)
MICRO-LABEL: Tiny labels (10px, IBM Plex Mono, uppercase, widest tracking)
SECTION-LABEL: Section identifiers (12px, IBM Plex Mono, uppercase, wider tracking)
BUTTON: Action text (12px, IBM Plex Mono, uppercase, wider tracking)
CHIP: Tag text (10px, IBM Plex Mono, uppercase, widest tracking)
