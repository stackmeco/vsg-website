Based on the typography audit provided in your files, here is the complete **Text Hierarchy Specification** for the Verifiable Systems Group (VSG) design system.

This hierarchy follows the **"Industrial Control Room"** aesthetic, prioritizing data density (14px base) and mechanical precision (monospaced labels).

### **1. Global Font Families**
* **Signal (Headings):** `Space Grotesk`
    * *Usage:* Page titles, section headers, major announcements.
    * *Vibe:* Geometric, technical, authoritative.
* **Context (Body):** `Manrope`
    * *Usage:* Paragraphs, descriptions, UI elements.
    * *Vibe:* Humanist, highly legible, neutral.
* **Data (Technical):** `IBM Plex Mono`
    * *Usage:* Timestamps, financial data, tags, micro-labels, code.
    * *Vibe:* Engineered, precise, machine-readable.

---

### **2. The Hierarchy Scale**

#### **Level 1: Display (Hero)**
Used for page titles and primary mission statements.
* **Class:** `PAGE-HERO`
* **Font:** Space Grotesk
* **Size:** `text-3xl` to `text-5xl` (40px – 56px)
* **Weight:** Bold (700)
* **Tracking:** Tight (`-0.03em`)
* **Line Height:** 1.0 - 1.1
* [cite_start]*Example:* "Engineering verifiable systems..." [cite: 2989]

#### **Level 2: Structural Headers**
Used to divide major sections of a page.
* **Class:** `SECTION-HEADER`
* **Font:** Space Grotesk
* **Size:** `text-xl` to `text-2xl` (24px – 32px)
* **Weight:** SemiBold (600)
* **Tracking:** Tight (`-0.02em`)
* [cite_start]*Example:* "How we verify value", "Legal and Operating Structure" [cite: 2991, 2998]

#### **Level 3: Component Headers**
Used for card titles, feature names, and sub-sections.
* **Class:** `CARD-TITLE` / `SUBSECTION-HEADER`
* **Font:** Space Grotesk
* **Size:** `text-base` to `text-lg` (16px – 18px)
* **Weight:** SemiBold (600)
* [cite_start]*Example:* "Manager", "Strategic Definition", "Axiom" [cite: 2992, 3007]

#### **Level 4: Body Text**
The core reading experience. Note the **14px base size** (smaller than the standard 16px) to create density.
* **Class:** `BODY-PARAGRAPH`
* **Font:** Manrope
* **Size:** `text-sm` (14px)
* **Weight:** Normal (400)
* **Line Height:** Relaxed or Normal
* **Color:** Muted Foreground (Silver/Gray)
* [cite_start]*Example:* Project descriptions, manifesto text. [cite: 2990]

#### **Level 5: Data & Telemetry**
Used for numbers, statistics, and financial metrics.
* **Class:** `DATA-VALUE`
* **Font:** IBM Plex Mono
* **Size:** `text-2xl` (32px) or `text-sm` (14px) depending on context
* **Weight:** Bold (700)
* **Feature:** `tabular-nums` (Ensures numbers align vertically)
* [cite_start]*Example:* "$2.4M", "847.2", System Clock [cite: 3006]

#### **Level 6: Micro-Labels (The "Architectural" Layer)**
Used for tags, section identifiers, and status indicators. This is crucial for the "Control Room" look.
* **Class:** `MICRO-LABEL` / `SECTION-LABEL`
* **Font:** IBM Plex Mono
* **Size:** `text-[10px]` (10px) or `text-xs` (12px)
* **Transform:** Uppercase
* **Tracking:** Widest (`0.2em`)
* [cite_start]*Example:* "SYSTEM STATUS", "IN THIS DOCUMENT", "01", "02" [cite: 2991, 2998]

---

### **3. UI Elements**

* **Navigation Links:**
    * `text-[10px]` | Mono | Uppercase | [cite_start]Tracking-Widest [cite: 3003]
* **Buttons:**
    * `text-xs` (12px) | Mono | Uppercase | [cite_start]Tracking-Wider [cite: 2990]
* **Chips / Tags:**
    * `text-[10px]` | Mono | Uppercase | [cite_start]Tracking-Widest [cite: 2991]
* **Input Labels:**
    * `text-sm` (14px) | Manrope | [cite_start]Medium [cite: 2994]

### **4. Color & Contrast Strategy**
* **Primary Text:** `text-foreground` (Off-white)
* **Secondary Text:** `text-muted-foreground` (Muted Silver - 75% opacity)
* [cite_start]**Accent Text:** `text-primary` (Signal Orange) - Used for active states, cursors, and critical data points. [cite: 2988]
