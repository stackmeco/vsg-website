This is the "Deep State" audit. We are looking for the things that are not just "wrong," but **sub-optimal** for a Tier-1 Engineering brand.

I have identified **5 Final "Ghost" Issues** that are still lingering in the code structure or micro-copy. These are the details that distinguish a "reskinned template" from a "proprietary interface."

-----

### **I. The "404" Page is Generic (The "Dead End" Risk)**

  * **Current State:** `client/src/pages/not-found.tsx` likely says "Page Not Found" with a standard button.
  * **The Vibe Kill:** It looks like a broken website.
  * **The Fix:** It should look like a **System Error**.
      * **Headline:** "404: SIGNAL LOST" or "INVALID VECTOR"
      * **Body:** " The requested path does not exist within the verified architecture."
      * **Button:** "Return to Console" (instead of "Back Home").

### **II. The "Read More" Link (The Blog Trope)**

  * **Current State:** In `InsightCard.tsx` and `Pipeline.tsx`, the links likely say **"Read more"** with an arrow.
  * **The Vibe Kill:** "Read more" is for blogs and news sites. Institutional tools don't ask you to "read"; they allow you to **"Access"** or **"View."**
  * **The Fix:** Change all instances of "Read more" to **"Access Entry"** or **"View Analysis"**. It sounds like you are opening a file, not a story.

### **III. The "Studio Metrics" Labels (The Defensive Tone)**

  * **Current State:** In `Overview.tsx` (Sidebar):
      * *"2 Founders (Full ownership)"* -\> Sounds defensive/proud.
      * *"100% Self-funded"* -\> Sounds like a bootstrap startup.
  * **The Fix:** Use **Balance Sheet Terms**.
      * Change "Full ownership" to **"Equity: Undiluted"**. (Financial term).
      * Change "100% Self-funded" to **"Capitalization: Proprietary"**. (Institutional term).

### **IV. The "Next Step" Softness**

  * **Current State:** In `NextStepBlock.tsx`:
      * *"See how Verified Systems operates..."*
      * *"Reach out if you'd like to connect."*
  * **The Vibe Kill:** "See how" and "like to connect" are conversational.
  * **The Fix:**
      * System: **"Review Operational Architecture."**
      * Contact: **"Initiate Communication Channel."**

### **V. The Browser Title (The Invisible Brand)**

  * **Current State:** `index.html` likely defaults to "Vite App" or just "Verified Systems."
  * **The Fix:** Set the `<title>` to: **"Verified Systems | Truth Engineering"**.
  * **Favicon:** Ensure the favicon isn't the Vite logo. Even a blank orange square is better than the default.
