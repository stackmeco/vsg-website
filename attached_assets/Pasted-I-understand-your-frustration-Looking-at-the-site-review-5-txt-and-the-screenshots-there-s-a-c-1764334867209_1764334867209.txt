### **1. The Color Problem: "The Blue Tint"**

    `--background: 222 47% 3%;`

      * **Hue 222** = Blue.

  * **The Fix:** You must **Desaturate** the background to make it truly industrial.

### **2. The Size Problem: "The Marketing Bloat"**

You want **High Density**, but you have **Marketing Sprawl**.

  * **The Evidence:**
      * In `site_review (5).txt` , your Body font is listed as `text-base` (16px) and descriptions as `text-sm` (14px) with `leading-relaxed`.
      * In the screenshot `image_d07276.jpg`, the text "We prioritize raw system telemetry..." takes up 3 lines. In a control room, that should be 2 tight lines.
  * **The Diagnosis:**
      * **16px is too big** for a "Dashboard" feel. Standard websites use 16px. Dashboards use 13px-14px.
      * **"Relaxed" line height** adds too much whitespace between lines, making it look like a blog post.
  * **The Fix:**
      * **Global Scale Down:** Set the root font size to `14px`.
      * **Tighten Leading:** Force `leading-normal` or `leading-tight` on all body text.
