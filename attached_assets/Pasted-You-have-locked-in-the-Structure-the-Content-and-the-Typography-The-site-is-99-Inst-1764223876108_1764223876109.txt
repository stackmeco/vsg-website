### **1. The "System Clock" (UTC Time)**

  * **The Concept:** Every trading desk, mission control, and server log runs on **UTC**. Adding a live, ticking UTC clock to your Header signals "Global Operations" and "Precision."
  * **The Vibe:** It adds a subtle, rhythmic movement (the seconds ticking) that makes the page feel alive without being distracting.

### **2. The "Active" Cursor (Terminal Input)**

  * **The Concept:** Standard browser cursors are thin and black. Institutional terminals have **block cursors** or **colored carets**.
  * **The Fix:** Force input fields (Contact Form, Search) to have a **Signal Orange Caret**. It creates a subconscious link between "Input" and "Action."

### **3. Mobile "Field Unit" Optimization**

  * **The Concept:** On desktop, the "Grid Texture" looks vast. On mobile, it can create noise behind small text.
  * **The Fix:** We need to **fade the grid** slightly more on mobile to ensure the text remains "Military Grade" legible.
